Digital Infrastructure & Climate Change

The digital infrastructure that powers our online world has a significant environmental footprint. Understanding this impact is the first step toward making more sustainable choices.

🏢

Data Centers

Global data centers consume ~200 TWh annually, about 1% of world electricity use.

📱

Device Production

Manufacturing a single smartphone requires mining rare earth elements with high environmental cost.

📶

Network Traffic

Global internet traffic generates significant CO2 emissions from constant data transmission.
