How Eco-Search Works

Understanding the simple yet powerful cycle of sustainable searching.

The Process Explained

1
🔍

User Searches

You enter your search query just like you would on any search engine. The results are powered by established search providers, ensuring quality and relevance.

2
📢

Ads Generate Revenue

Relevant advertisements appear alongside your search results. These ads generate revenue through pay-per-click advertising, just like traditional search engines.

3
💰

Revenue Funds Projects

The search engine allocates a significant portion (often 80-100%) of its advertising revenue to environmental projects and charities.

4
🌍

Projects Create Impact

Funded projects focus on reforestation, ocean cleanup, renewable energy, and other sustainability initiatives that directly combat climate change.
