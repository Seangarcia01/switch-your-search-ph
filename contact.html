Frequently Asked Questions

Are eco-search engines as effective as Google?

Yes! Most eco-search engines use the same underlying search technology (like Bing or Google) to deliver accurate results while adding their environmental layer on top.

Is my data safe with eco-search engines?

Absolutely. Many eco-search engines prioritize user privacy and don't sell your data to advertisers. Ecosia, for example, explicitly states they don't create personal profiles of their users.

How many trees can I plant by searching?

Approximately 45 searches on Ecosia funds the planting of one tree. With 3-4 daily searches, you could help plant 25-30 trees per year!

Can I use eco-search engines at work?

Yes! Many eco-search engines offer business versions or browser extensions that make it easy for entire organizations to switch their search habits collectively.
